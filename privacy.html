Barober: Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Data Protection

We employ strict security measures to protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you register or book a service through our app.

No Data Sharing for Marketing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties for advertising or promotional purposes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register or book a service through our app:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location (used for assigning the nearest available barber)

2. Payment Information

All payments are securely processed via Razorpay, a trusted third-party payment gateway. We do not collect or store your credit/debit card information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Booking and managing services
  • Assigning barbers based on your location
  • Sending confirmations, alerts, and updates
  • Customer support and feedback
  • Service improvements

4. Sharing of Information

We share limited information with the assigned barber only during the service period to facilitate smooth delivery:

  • Location (for navigation)
  • Phone Number (for direct communication)

Once the service is completed, this data is no longer accessible to the barber.

5. Data Security

We employ strict security measures to protect your personal information. While we follow best practices, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. No Data Sharing for Marketing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties for advertising or promotional purposes.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request to view the data we hold
  • Ask for corrections to your information
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally applicable)

To make such requests, contact us using the details below.
